About SMVIT

SMVIT is a well-known institute with a sprawling 133-acre campus in Bengaluru. It offers excellent infrastructure including labs, hostels, sports facilities, library, cafeteria, medical facilities, and transport.

The institute offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including CSE, ECE, Mechanical, AI & ML, MBA, MCA, M.Tech, Pharmacy, Architecture, and more. Active campus life includes cultural events, sports, and tech clubs.
